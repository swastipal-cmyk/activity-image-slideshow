Activity images — ranks 1–8

For each activity, all eight ranks appear together. Rank 1 is highlighted in green. Activities are in a random order each time you build. Click your favourite (or press keys 1–8) to compare with the sheet’s top rank — then the view moves to the next activity automatically (last activity stays put).
